Tandoor Chicken

Serving suggestion

Ingredients

  • 15ml Olive oil
  • 125ml yoghurt
  • Squeeze of lemon juice
  • 5ml curry powder
  • 5ml ginger
  • 5ml coriander
  • 5ml paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Mix the olive oil, yoghurt and lemon juice together with the curry powder, ginger, coriander and paprika.
  2. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Marinate overnight.
  4. BBQ chicken over medium coals.

Serving Suggestions:

Serve with salad tossed in Yorgurt & Herb Salad dressing and hot naan bread.
