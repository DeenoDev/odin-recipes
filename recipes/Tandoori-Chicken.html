Tandoor Chicken

Serving suggestion

Ingredients

  • 15ml Olive oil
  • 125ml yoghurt
  • Squeeze of lemon juice
  • 5ml curry powder
  • 5ml ginger
  • 5ml coriander
  • 5ml paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Mix the cheeses with an egg in a bowl, and set aside.
  2. Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop.
  3. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
  5. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9Ã—13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  6. Top the noodles with some of the cheese mixture.
  7. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  8. Cover with foil and bake.
  9. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan and bake another 15 minutes.

Important tip:

Let the lasagna sit/rest for at least 15 minutes once removed from the oven (even 30-45 minutes is fine). This will keep it from becoming runny and help it to keep its shape when cut. Resting is not required when reheating.
